**Northwest Community Bail Fund (NCBF)** \\
"The Northwest Community Bail Fund (NCBF) provides cash bail for marginalized people charged with crimes who are unable to afford bail and find themselves incarcerated while awaiting routine court appearances in King, Snohomish, and Pierce Counties in Washington State. This allows people to get out of jail during their trial period and defend themselves from a position of freedom." \\ 
https://www.nwcombailfund.org/who-we-are/
